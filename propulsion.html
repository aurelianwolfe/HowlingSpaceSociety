Chemical Kinetics & Energy Density Archive

LV-01

KEROLOX

RP-1 (Highly refined kerosene) + LOX. The gold standard for first-stage boosters due to its high density and ease of handling.

ISP (Sea Level): ~280s
Density: High
Main Use: Falcon 9, Atlas V
LV-02

METHALOX

Liquid Methane + LOX. Ideal for reusability as it leaves zero soot in the engines. Crucial for Mars ISRU missions.

ISP (Vacuum): ~380s
Coking: None
Main Use: Starship, Vulcan
LV-03

HYDROLOX

Liquid Hydrogen + LOX. The most efficient chemical propellant. Extremely low density but incredibly high exhaust velocity.

ISP (Vacuum): ~450s
Handling: Difficult (Cryo)
Main Use: SLS, Ariane 5

Oxidizer Analysis: Liquid Oxygen (LOX)

Liquid Oxygen is the standard oxidizer for nearly all modern orbital rockets. It must be kept at cryogenic temperatures ($ -183^\circ\text{C} $). When mixed with fuel in the combustion chamber, it facilitates the rapid expansion of gases required for thrust.

In the **Howling Space Society**, we analyze the molecular mass of exhaust products to determine the efficiency of the burn.
